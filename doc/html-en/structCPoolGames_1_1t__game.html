◆ winWeight

t_weight CPoolGames::t_game::winWeight

Weight to determine the winner.

Weight is determined by dividing with remainder 256-bit random numbers on the weight of the game + 1n (that is never equal to 0) To search for the winner of the backend needs to take addresses of all the members of the pool, sort them in alphabetical order and consistently adding up the weight of the participants to find someone, adding weight which result >=winWeight And <=winWeight, he will be the winners Participants who entered the pool in violation of the restrictions (for example, less than minSeconds before the end of the game) will have for the current game, the weight is 0 and thus excluded from the winners.

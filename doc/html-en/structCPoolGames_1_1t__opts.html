◆ maxDeposit

nat CPoolGames::t_opts::maxDeposit

Maximum deposit for the pool.

A pool with the algorithm AlgoTimeVol can allow the owner of a huge deposit to enter at the last moment and with a high probability take the rewards. To limit the size of deposits in the pool to reasonable limits and give users comparable chances this parameter together with minDeposit will allow you to get a fair draw. 0 - there is no limit
